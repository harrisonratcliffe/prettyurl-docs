# How to shorten a URL

This guide will show you how to shorten a URL.

1. Begin by navigating to the PrettyURL website at https://prettyurl.net/.

2. Click on the "Dashboard" button at the top-right corner and sign in or create a new account.

3. Once logged in, select "Links" from the left-hand navigation menu.

4. Click on the "Shorten Link" button to start creating a new shortened URL.

5. Paste or type the URL you want to shorten into the provided input field.

6. Adding an optional title to help you organize and quickly identify your links within your dashboard.

7. Choose a suitable domain from the dropdown menu to use in your short URL.

8. Click "Create" to generate your shortened link.

You can now share your link and begin tracking link visits.
